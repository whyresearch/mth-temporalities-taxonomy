## 1. Purpose

| # | Project | Purpose |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | Tracing matters of scale… | Walking and thinking-with stones, minerals and fossils to challenge linear, human-centric timescales. Performative walk. |
| 2 | Everywhere, all at once | Designing Augmented Reality experiences to allow the interrogation of multiple temporalities that foster engagement with MtH worlds. |
| 3 | Leaky Cups | Connect human with local temporalities of water (flow rate from surface monitoring station) between now and 30-years ago (historical value). Critiques assumptions about progress. |
| 4 | Mold Sounds | Design of an artefact to listen to growth of mould over time. |
| 5 | Being The Creek | Experiencing historical events through Creeks' perspective. |
| 6 | Bio-Digital Calendar | Build intimate interactions between a user and kombucha through sonification and active listening. |
| 7 | Designing with more-than-human temporalities | Engage with ecosystems, and a range of inhabitants (from human festival goers to plants and natural species). |
| 8 | Caring through | Provides a typology informed by different kinds of care based on MtH temporalities. |
| 9 | Designing for Interdependence… | Garden observations and the making of a beehouse mobilizing care for the local ecosystem. |
| 10 | Sensing Bodies | Highlight reciprocal people-plant relationships and foster a deeper sensibility toward colonial histories of local landscapes. |
| 11 | Felt Experiences with Kombucha Scoby | Explore first person perspectives through design research with Kombucha Scoby. |
| 12 | Metamorphonic | Crafting a design habitat for silk moth caterpillars allowing audience to listen to growth and change over time. |
| 13 | Cyano-chromic Interface | Microbe-embedded artefact challenging human perception of time and gradual change at the microscopic scale. |
| 14 | Me, the Hill and My Browser | Attune humans with local nature's temporalities. |
| 15 | Phenology Probes | Developing a method to study the multifaceted relations between humans and nature and how they are manifested in a particular time and place. |
| 16 | Camera Traps | Using camera trap to deepen human/nature environment in gardens. |
| 17 | High Water Pants | Making climate change tangible through speculative wearable technology that 'bends' time. |
